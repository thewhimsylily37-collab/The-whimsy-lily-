Our Story

Two hands, one hook, a whole lot of heart.

The Whimsy Lily began at a kitchen table — a mother teaching her daughter the first chain stitch, and a daughter refusing to let the craft stay a hobby. What started as an afternoon pastime grew into a shared belief: that handmade art has a place in every home, not just the ones that can afford a gallery piece.

Every piece we make passes through both sets of hands. One of us designs, the other refines; one dyes the yarn, the other counts the stitches. It's slow work, and we like it that way.

— with love, the Whimsy Lily duo
